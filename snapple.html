Snapple Asia Products

Jennie Cortés was managing, designing and developing the project of Snapple Asia and Snapple LATAM. This was a massive project with multiple websites for different locations such as: Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Hong Kong, South Korea, Chile, Costa Rica, Panama, Puerto Rico and more.

For this project I was tasked with the product section of multiple locations. I had to apply the correct list of products for the different locations and create their specific SEO for each of the products.

This was an amazing project to be part of and have the experience of working from Puerto Rico with Jennie Cortés while she was in South Korea. The big scope of this project and the different collaborations makes me very happy that I was part of it.

This project was done for HIT Interactive Marketing Group.
