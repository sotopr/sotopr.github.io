Tienda Ediciones SM

After years in the market they wanted to redesign their online store to a simpler one that is accessible and easy to navigate for customers in search of their physical or digital books needs.

They wanted an easy navigation to directly display their existing book categories, no distractions, just books. They already had their informative site and just needed to redesign their website, their color palatte is simple white on red. We connected the store with their own internal data management service in order to properly display their products with real time prices and information.

It was insteresting creating their site after using books published through them when I studied back in school. This project was done for Websoft PR.
