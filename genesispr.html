Genesis Puerto Rico

Genesis Puerto Rico a new automobile brand to enter the Puerto Rico market, they needed a digital presence to be known and attract users online to increase sales.

After the success that was the launch of Hyundai Puerto Rico, I was given the oppotunity to manage the development of the new website for Genesis Puerto Rico. Given their more modern and professional design, I worked directly with their internal team to create the best experience that represented their brand following their global guidelines, creating a visually impactful and expresive website.

It has been a magnificent experience being able to help establish a digital presence of a new automobile brand in the market.

This project was done for Websoft PR.
