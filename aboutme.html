About Me

Hey! Carlos here, web developer from the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. During my bachelor's degree in digital graphic design I discovered a new love for user interface and user experience design. Since graduating the focus of my career has been UI/UX design and Front-end development.

Through my career I have worked with over 40 clients from different industries giving them support, maintenance and feedback to their websites. I have worked directly on the design, development and launch of over 25 websites, not counting the landings pages or promotional pages. With my professional experience, interpersonal skills and charisma I have been able to deliver and meet the expectations of clients creating friendships along the way.

People like to use intuitive products in their everyday life, I want to create new and familiar experiences for people to enjoy.
