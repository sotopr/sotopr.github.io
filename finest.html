Pan Pepin Finest

Pan Pepín Finest is a branch of higher quality breads from Pan Pepín catalog. They are a very popular bakery company in Puerto Rico with other types of products. They wanted to create a more informative website about their product line and include recipes for each product with instructions on how to make them.

I was tasked to create a front page that had to include all 4 products of Pan Pepín Finest using the designs of each product page made by one of the creative designers. After the design process was completed, I began the development of the front page and the individual pages of each product including their respective SEO. This website was develop using HTML and CSS.

It was very fun to work with because of the uniqueness of each product and their different recipes. This project was done for HIT Interactive Marketing Group.
